FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can a Diploma nurse from Ghana apply to write the NCLEX?
+
Yes — Diploma nurses from Ghana can apply to write the NCLEX. However, the eligibility depends on the state board you apply to. Some states (like Montana and Nevada) are more accessible to diploma nurses. We assess your qualifications on your discovery call and recommend the best state for your background.
How long does the entire process take from registration to NCLEX?
+
The full process typically takes 12–18 months from start to exam day. Credential evaluation alone takes 8–12 weeks with Trumerit or CGFNS. Board application processing takes 4–8 weeks. We begin your NCLEX coaching in parallel so you are ready the moment your ATT arrives. Timelines may vary slightly by country of origin.
Where can African nurses write the NCLEX exam?
+
African nurses can write the NCLEX at Pearson VUE testing centres across Africa (Ghana, Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya), as well as the UK, USA, Canada, and many other countries. You do not need to travel to the US to sit the exam. We help you book your exam at the most convenient location.
How much does the full process cost?
+
Our service fees are separate from the third-party fees (Trumerit, Pearson VUE, state board). Our packages start from GHS 1,200 for coaching only up to GHS 2,500 for the full package. Third-party costs typically add $500–$900 USD. We give you a full transparent cost breakdown on your discovery call — no hidden fees.
Do you help with Midwives and Public Health Nurses — not just RNs?
+
Yes. We have specialist pathways for Registered Midwives (RM) and Registered Public Health Nurses (RPHN). The process differs from standard RN applications and we have coaches with specific expertise in these pathways. Book a discovery call to discuss your specific qualification.
I'm already abroad in the UK — can I still use NCLEX Pro Solutions?
+
Absolutely. A significant portion of our clients are diaspora nurses in the UK, US, and Canada who want to convert their African nursing qualification. All our coaching is available remotely via WhatsApp video, Zoom, and Google Meet. We run evening and weekend sessions to work around your shift pattern.
What happens if I fail my NCLEX after coaching with you?
+
We stand by our clients. If you complete the full coaching programme and do not pass, we will work with you at a reduced retaker rate to prepare for your retest. We analyse your NCLEX performance report together, identify gaps, and rebuild your strategy. Our 95% first-attempt pass rate speaks to how seriously we take preparation.
