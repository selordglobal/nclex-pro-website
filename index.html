Frequently Asked Questions
How long does credential evaluation take? ▾
Credential evaluation through Trumerit or CGFNS typically takes 8–12 weeks. We monitor your application daily and will notify you immediately of any updates.
What documents do I need to submit? ▾
You need: valid passport, NMC licence, nursing certificate, official transcripts, and birth certificate. Upload them in the Documents section of your portal.
How do I change my target country? ▾
Update your target country in Settings → Application. If you have already started the credential evaluation process, contact our support team first.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind? ▾
Refund eligibility depends on the stage of your application. Contact our support team via WhatsApp to discuss your situation.
How do I change my temporary password? ▾
Go to Settings → Security → Change Password. Enter your new password and confirm it. Your password will be updated immediately.
